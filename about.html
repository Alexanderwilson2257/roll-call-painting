About Roll Call Painting

Family-owned, locally operated, and proudly serving the Greater Phoenix Area since 2013.

Our Story

Roll Call Painting was founded in 2013 by Marcus and Dana Rivera, two Phoenix natives with a passion for craftsmanship and a commitment to their community. What started as a two-person operation painting homes in the Chandler area has grown into a full-service painting company serving residential and commercial clients across the entire Greater Phoenix metro area.

The name "Roll Call Painting" reflects our belief that every project deserves a team that shows up, ready to work, every single day. We take attendance seriously — both literally and figuratively. When we schedule a job, we are there on time, every time, with the right crew and the right materials.

Over the past decade we have completed more than 2,000 projects ranging from single-room touch-ups to full commercial repaints. Our reputation is built entirely on word of mouth and five-star reviews, which means every project we take on is treated as an opportunity to earn your trust.

What We Stand For

Quality First

We never cut corners. From surface preparation to the final coat, every step is done right. We use only premium paints from Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore.

Honest Pricing

Our estimates are detailed, transparent, and free. What we quote is what you pay — no surprise charges at the end of the job.

Respect for Your Space

We treat every home and business as if it were our own. We protect your furniture, clean up after ourselves, and minimize disruption to your daily routine.
