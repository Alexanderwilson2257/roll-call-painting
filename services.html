Our Painting Services

Interior, exterior, and commercial painting solutions for every budget and project size.

Interior Painting

Our interior painting services cover every surface inside your home or business. Whether you need a single accent wall refreshed or a full multi-room repaint, our team works efficiently and carefully to deliver a flawless finish. We protect your floors, furniture, and fixtures throughout the entire process.

Interior services include walls, ceilings, trim and baseboards, doors and door frames, cabinets, and accent features. We offer color consultation at no extra charge to help you find the perfect palette for your space.

Exterior Painting

Arizona's intense heat and UV exposure can fade and crack exterior paint quickly. Roll Call Painting uses premium exterior-grade paints specifically formulated to withstand desert conditions and protect your home's surfaces year after year. We pressure wash and properly prepare every surface before applying a single drop of paint.

Exterior services include siding, stucco, trim and fascia, fences, gates, decks, and garage doors. We also offer wood rot repair and caulking as part of a full exterior preparation package.

Commercial Painting

We understand that business downtime costs money. Our commercial painting crews are available for early morning, evening, and weekend scheduling to minimize disruption to your operations. We work with property managers, HOAs, retail businesses, and office complexes throughout the Phoenix metro area.

Commercial services include offices, retail stores, warehouses, multi-unit residential complexes, HOA common areas, and parking structures. We provide detailed project timelines and progress updates throughout every commercial job.
